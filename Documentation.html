Documentation About The Game

Introduction About The Game

This Game Is A Cars Race And To Play iT You Just Need To
Use The Keys ( Up , Down , Left , Right )
To Move The car .

How To Play The Game

First Of All You Have To Press The Butoon Of Start To Start Playing The Game
Then You Have To Use The KeyBoard Keys To Move The Car
Up : Moves The Car Up
Down : Moves The Car Down
Left : Moves The Cars In The Left Side
Right: Moves The Car In The Right Side .

Restrictions Of The Game

When You Start Moving The Car , There Will Be Opposite Cars Facing You If One Of Them Touch Or Hit Your
Car The Game Stops And You Lose
Try To Avoid These Opposit Cars .

After Finshing The Game

After Finshing The Game You Will Find The Score Of
Pointes That You Scored And A Button To Restart The
Game If You Want .
